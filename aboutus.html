VISION - WEDSITE LOVE HISTORY MISSION: AFFIRMING THE IMPORTANCE OF WORLD CULTURAL HISTORY - ENHANCED VIETNAM BRANDS.

Starting from the idea of bringing the cultural history of Vietnam in particular and the world in general to introduce to friends at home and abroad, our team has researched, developed and released the LOVE HISTORY wedsite product, going through the journey. Despite the difficult development, LOVE HISTORY was established in 2021 with the dream of building a world leading historical discovery tourism brand. Since then LOVE HISTORY has grown continuously, by 2022 LOVE HISTORY has developed and gradually affirmed its position in the field of historical and cultural tourism experience.

2. LOVE HISTORY'S MISSION

ALOVE HISTORY's mission is "Bring Vietnamese brand products with good quality, good service to customers in regions of Vietnam and around the world". Yody wishes to bring to all customers around the world outstanding landmarks and historical places and create the most favorable conditions for customers to come and experience those places brought by Vietnamese people. Regardless of class, regardless of rich or poor, customers who have never experienced services beyond expectations, everyone will be welcomed and respected when coming to LOVE STORY. Therefore, Yody diligently researches and brings the best products and tours in both price and quality to customers. Along with that, Love Story always spends most of its time training the culture of service for all employees. Each employee will be a brand ambassador, each employee will be a model of Love Story's service culture and give the best value to each customer.
